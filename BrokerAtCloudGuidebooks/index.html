Welcome to the Broker@Cloud Guide Books

The Adopter's Guides to Broker@Cloud framework, mechanisms and tools

Broker@Cloud is offering a framework, services, mechanisms and tools for different quality assurance and optimization tasks on cloud service brokerage platforms along the service lifecycle.

Different adaptation needs require knowledge about different aspects of the Broker@Cloud offerings. Broker@Cloud Guide Books are focusing on offering assistance to the following most common use cases:

  • Cloud Service Brokerage Platform: Broker@Cloud enablement of a cloud service brokerage platform
  • Cloud Service Broker Ecosystem: Operating a Broker@Cloud-enabled cloud service brokerage platform
  • Broker@Cloud Framework: Extending and adopting Broker@Cloud beyond current offerings
